Spanakopita

Description

Spanakopita (meaning Spinach pie is the most famous Greek pie.

It is a typical traditional Greek pie with many layers, spinach and often, feta cheese.

Ingredients

  • Spinach
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Pastry sheet
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Put the spinach leaves in boiled water.
  2. Wait some minutes and remove the leaves.
  3. Mix the leaves with feta cheese, onion, olive oil and seasoning.
  4. On a baking pan, lay the pastry sheet and rinse with olive oil.
  5. Pour some spinach mix (step 3 above), lay some pastry sheet, and repeat as many times as necessary.
  6. Bake in the oven for 2 hours on low heat.
  7. Bon appetit!
