Spaghetti

Description

This Greek pasta dish is bursting with flavor: Classic Greek Spaghetti with Meat Sauce is made with luscious ground beef and tomato sauce, along with cheese-coated spaghetti.

This dish of Greek spaghetti with meat sauce is known as Makaronia me kima in Greek. This dish is a favorite among most Greeks, adults, and children alike. Nobody can resist makaronia me kima, especially its homemade version.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Mashed meat
  • Onions
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta and then put it to the dryer
  2. Cook the mashed meat on hot olive oil
  3. Put the tomato sauce together with the mashed meat for 30 minutes
  4. Mix the pasta with the cooked meat
  5. Serve on a plate and sprinkle with cheese of your liking
  6. Bon appetit!
